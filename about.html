About Me

"One must grow or else pay more for remaining the same." Norman Mailer once said this many years ago, and as the years pass by, it has become more and more apposite. With technology growing at an exponential rate, businesses cannot remain stagnant without disrupting their top and bottom line. They must ride the wave of technological innovation and I hope to build a career developing the technologies that induce those waves.

Currently, I am a student at the University of Michigan in the College of Literature, Science, and the Arts studying computer science and economics. But more importantly, I am an analytical and creative thinker capable of identifying issues and evaluating unique solutions. My passion is problem solving and my career interests lie in strategy consulting, financial analytics and financial technology.

Please feel free to reach out to me!
